Light To Life
Join us in the First Annual Light To Life Lighting + Design Conference for Lighting Consultants, Interior & Exterior Designers, Facility Managers, Architects and more! Engage in thought-sharing and strategic discussions in this exciting conference where key issues in the lighting industry will be highlighted. With the theme of 'Light to Life', the conference will look into providing an open platform for industry professionals to present, discuss, explore and exchange the optimal practices and emerging concepts of the lighting industry with architecture and design in mind.

Session 1 Speaker
Mr. K.Seshardi | CEO, Gritti Consulting

Presentations by K.Seshadri (SESH) are known to be with ‘light’ humour and good illustrations that help the audience enjoy while learning. He is CEO of Gritti Consulting Pte Ltd, Chairman (SEA & Middle East) of Luma Group Lighting and ex-VP of Philips Asia Pacific. He also contributes as chairman, SPRING Technical Committee for Lamps and Related equipment. Sesh is a part-time lecturer at NUS Department of Building (School of Design & Environment) and Adjunct Lecturer at BCA Academy for Green Mark Manager, Green Mark Professional and Singapore Certified Energy Manager programs.

Session 2 Speaker
Mr. Martin Klaasen | Director, Klaasen Lighting Design

Martin is an award-winning lighting designer, design consultant, lecturer and resource speaker with more than 30 years of experience in the lighting industry operating predominantly in the Asia Pacific regin. Martin has a Master's degree in industrial design and started his career of many years in creative and innovative lighting design with Philips (in Holland and overseas). Since 2011, Martin applies his passion for lighting design exclusively through his own company; Klaasen Lighting Design (KLD).

Session 3 Speaker
Mr. Kenny Eng | Director, Nyee Phoe Group

Kenny graduated from the National University of Singapore in 1999 with a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Real Estate. As the fourth generation of the Nyee Phoe family business, Kenny practically grew up in gardens and greens. The horticulture legacy turns a century old in 2011; its values deeply ingrained in the aspiring entrepreneur’s mind. In 2000, Kenny created Gardenasia with the original intent of having a sales arm for Nyee Phoe’s business. However, after he recognized the potential of the lifestyle industry, Kenny realigned the company’s direction to create holistic experiences as a means to re-introduce perspectives of life. Kenny’s tireless efforts at constant reinvention were recognized with the Spirit of Enterprise Award in 2005.
